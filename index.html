About the team race

The Ultrabalaton Running Fest is an annual running event in Hungary that encircles Lake Balaton which is the largest freshwater lake in Europe. The course is unique and provides runners with stunning views of Lake Balaton and the surrounding region's cultural and historical landmarks.

The race can be completed as a team or as an individual runner and has a 32-hour time limit. Runners traversing a distance of 221 kilometers , which is more than 5 marathon distances.

Team coordination

In the team category, each team is made up of up to 14 members, who take turns to run each section of the 54-stage course. The team format adds an element of strategy and teamwork to the race.
